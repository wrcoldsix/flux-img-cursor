Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AI image generation

What is AI image generation?

AI image generation is a technology that uses artificial intelligence to create images from text descriptions. It employs advanced machine learning models trained on vast datasets to understand and visualize your text prompts.

How long does it take to generate an image?

Image generation typically takes 10-30 seconds per image. The exact time depends on the complexity of your prompt, the number of images being generated, and current server load.

What types of images can I create?

You can create a wide variety of images including digital art, realistic photos, illustrations, landscapes, portraits, abstract art, and more. The system supports various styles and artistic directions.

How can I get better results?

To get better results, try to be specific in your prompts. Include details about style, lighting, composition, and mood. You can also use artistic references and technical terms to guide the AI's output.

Can I edit the generated images?

Yes, you can download the generated images and edit them using any image editing software. You can also use our built-in tools to regenerate variations or modify specific parts of the images.
